01 About

A psychologist's eye,
a builder's hands.

I'm Indranil — I think in systems and work with people. My background is in psychology, but I've spent years applying it to the messiest parts of organisations: how teams break down, why the wrong people get hired, where friction turns productive and where it doesn't.

Alongside consulting work, I build tools that operationalise these ideas — including AI-driven products for B2B outreach and hiring intelligence. I believe the most useful work sits at the intersection of human behaviour and system design.
